About Us

Hoyu Group is a Malaysian company specializing in tire services and recycling solutions, comprising two main subsidiaries: Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd and Hoyu Recycling and Retreading Sdn Bhd. Hoyu Tayar offers high-quality tires and related services for various types of vehicles, including passenger cars, SUVs, commercial trucks, and buses. Hoyu Recycling and Retreading provides eco-friendly tire waste management solutions through advanced technologies and processes, such as tire retreading, to promote environmental sustainability. The company is dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service..

Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd

Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd is a well-established tire company based in Malaysia. The company has been in the industry for more than 30 years, providing high-quality tires and excellent customer service to both individual and commercial clients.

One of the company's main strengths is its wide range of tire products that cater to different types of vehicles, including passenger cars, SUVs, commercial trucks, and buses. Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd offers a diverse selection of tire brands, including leading international brands and their own in-house brand.

Aside from their tire products, Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd also provides other related services such as tire maintenance, repair, and replacement. They have a team of experienced and knowledgeable technicians who can assist customers with any tire-related issues they may have.

Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd is committed to delivering exceptional customer service, and they strive to build long-lasting relationships with their clients. They ensure that every customer receives personalized attention and that their needs are met with prompt and efficient service.

Hoyu Recycling and Retreading Sdn Bhd

Hoyu Recycling and Retreading Sdn Bhd is a subsidiary of Hoyu Tayar Sdn Bhd that specializes in tire recycling and retreading services. The company was established in 2012 and has since become a leading player in the tire retreading industry in Malaysia.

The company's core mission is to provide sustainable and eco-friendly solutions for tire waste management. They achieve this by utilizing advanced technologies and processes that allow them to recycle and retread used tires, reducing the amount of waste that ends up in landfills.

One of Hoyu Recycling and Retreading's main services is tire retreading, which involves the process of replacing worn-out treads on used tires to extend their lifespan. The company uses state-of-the-art retreading equipment and high-quality materials to ensure that their retreaded tires meet or even exceed the performance of brand new tires.

Hoyu Recycling and Retreading is committed to promoting environmental sustainability through their tire waste management services. By providing cost-effective and eco-friendly solutions, they help reduce the environmental impact of tire waste while also providing value to their customers.
